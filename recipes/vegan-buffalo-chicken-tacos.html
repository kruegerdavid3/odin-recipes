Vegan Buffalo Chicken Tacos

  • Prep Time: 15 min
  • Cook Time: 20 min
  • Total Time: 35 min

Description

These vegan buffalo chicken tacos are spicy, tangy, and packed with flavor. Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a fun party dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup soy chunks
  • 1/2 cup buffalo sauce
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Toritllas
  • Shredded cabbage, diced tomatoes, chopped onion and avocado for topping

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, soak the soy chunks in hot water for 10 minutes. Drain and squeeze out excess water.
  2. In a skillet, heat oil over medium heat. Add the soy chunks and cook until golden brown.
  3. Add the buffalo sauce, garlic powder, nutritional yeast, salt, and pepper. Stir well to coat the soy chunks.
  4. Warm the tortillas in a separate skillet or microwave.
  5. Assemble the tacos by placing the buffalo soy chunks on the tortillas and topping with shredded cabbage, diced tomatoes, chopped onion, and avocado.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Notes

You can adjust the spiciness of the tacos by adding more or less buffalo sauce. Feel free to add your favorite toppings!

Tips

For a creamier texture, you can add vegan sour cream or cashew cream on top of the tacos.

Variations

You can substitute the soy chunks with jackfruit or tempeh for a different texture and flavor.

Serving Suggestions

These tacos pair well with a side of guacamole and tortilla chips or a fresh salad.
