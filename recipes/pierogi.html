Pierogi

Description

This is a basic pierogi recipe. It is a traditional Polish dumpling that can be filled with various ingredients, including potatoes, cheese, and onions. The dough is made from flour, water, and eggs, and the filling can be customized to your liking. Pierogi are typically boiled and then pan-fried for a crispy texture.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 6 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups cold water divided
  • 2 large eggs beated
  • 6 tablespoons oil
  • 2 tsp salt

Filling

  • 3 3/4 pounds baking potatoes
  • 1 medium white onion finely diced
  • 1/3 cup salted butter softened, divided
  • 4 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese finely shredded
  • salt to taste
  • white pepper to taste, or very fine black pepper

For Serving

  • 1 onion diced or thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp salted butter or as needed for frying
  • sour cream optional, for serving
  • fresh chives optional, for serving

Instructions

Dough

  1. In a large bowl add flour, eggs, oil, salt, and 1 Â½ cups of water. Mix well to form a dough, adding more water if needed
  2. Once the dough is formed, knead it on a floured surface for about 5 minutes until smooth
  3. Cover the dough in plastic wrap and let it rest for 30 minutes

Filling

  1. Peel potatoes and cut in small cubes. Boil for ~15 min. or until fork tender
  2. while potatoes are cooking, cook onions in butter over medium/low heat
  3. Once potatoes are cooked, drain and mash them. Add onions, cheese, and any remaining butter. Continue mashing until very smooth

Assembly

  1. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to about 1/8 inch thick
  2. Cut out circles using a round cutter or glass, about 3 inches in diameter
  3. Place a small amount of filling in the center of each circle
  4. Fold the dough over the filling and pinch the edges to seal
  5. Repeat until all dough and filling are used

Cooking

  1. Cook more onions on med/low for serving
  2. Cook pierogi in pot of salted water until they float, typically 2-4 min
  3. Transfer pierogi to hot skillet and cook until browned on each side, 5 min
